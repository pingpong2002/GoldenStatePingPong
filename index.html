About

Matchmeds connects low income patients who can’t afford their prescriptions at retail prices to clinics that can help.

Affordable Prices

You pay based on your ability to pay, which is determined by annual income and family size.

Convenience

We deliver medication straight to your house. That’s one less errand on your to-do list.

Quality

Medications are ensured to be safe and legitimate as they are handled and received by certified safety-net clinics and charitable pharmacies.
