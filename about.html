About

Golden State Table Tennis is a full time table tennis club located in Milpitas, CA. We have eight tables, as well as professional grade flooring and good lighting. Our mission is to foster passion in the sport of table tennis and help kids and adults get the most out of their experience, whether it is to get a good workout or to win tournaments. We also offer a variety of programs, including private lessons, group lessons, as well as camps for children during holidays.
